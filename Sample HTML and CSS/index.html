F L O W E R

Flowers in India have rich cultural and symbolic significance and are used in celebrations, festivals, and prayers.




The Lotus represents majesty, fertility, riches, richness, knowledge, and peace, in addition to purity, beauty, and elegance

ROSE


Roses are beautiful flowers often used to convey love and affection.

TULIP


Tulips are known for their vibrant colors and are often associated with spring.

SUNFLOWER


Sunflowers are large, bright flowers that symbolize happiness and positivity.
